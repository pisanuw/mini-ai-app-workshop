the one big idea

You can now build software just by describing it.

No coding. You explain what you want in plain English, and the computer writes it for you.

the tool

This is Claude.

An AI that has read an enormous amount of writing. It's very good at turning your words into working things: explanations, drafts, and yes, little web apps.

how it works, in one breath

Like a very well-read assistant.

It predicts what should come next, so it can write it for you. Ask clearly and you get more of what you want. One catch: it sounds sure even when it's wrong, so double-check anything that matters.

quick definition

A "web app" is just a little program in your browser.

🧮 a tip splitter
🍲 a recipe box
⏳ a countdown
❓ a family quiz

If it opens in a browser, it's a web app. That's all we're making today.
